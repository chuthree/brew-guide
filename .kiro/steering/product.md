# Brew Guide - Product Overview

Brew Guide (咖啡冲煮指南) is an all-in-one coffee brewing assistant app that helps coffee enthusiasts manage their brewing process, coffee bean inventory, and tasting notes.

## Core Features

- **Brewing Timer**: Multi-stage guided brewing with visual pour indicators, supporting various equipment (V60, Clever Dripper, Origami, Kalita Wave, etc.)
- **Coffee Bean Management**: Inventory tracking with origin, processing method, variety, roast level, freshness monitoring, and consumption tracking
- **Brewing Notes**: Detailed records with ratings, flavor profiles, and equipment/method associations
- **Cloud Sync**: WebDAV and S3-compatible storage sync options
- **Multi-platform**: Web (PWA), iOS, Android, and desktop (macOS/Windows via Tauri)

## Target Users

Coffee enthusiasts who want to:

- Track and optimize their brewing process
- Manage coffee bean inventory and freshness
- Record and analyze brewing results over time
- Access their data across multiple devices

## Key UX Considerations

- Offline-first with PWA support
- Dark/light mode theming
- E-ink display optimization
- Safe area handling for mobile devices
- Haptic feedback on supported devices
